A flash back memory?




















































In November 2017, I went to Davao Oriental
for the first time to visit my late grandfather
(father's side) cemetery. When we went there
from Tagum, we took a break and I took
a picture on the side of the road because the
view was beautiful.
In this past February 2023, it was my older
sister's birthday, and they decided that we
should celebrate in Davao Oriental. When we
were there, once again, we took a break beside
the road, then I remembered that it was the
same spot that I went there for the first time,
and I got an Idea to recreate my pic there.
